Water Cycle

four stages

  • Evaporation
  • condensation
  • precipitation
  • collection

Condensation

This is when water that falls from the clouds as rain, snow, hail or sleet, collects in the oceans, rivers, lakes, streams. Most will infiltrate (soak into) the ground and will collect as underground water.
