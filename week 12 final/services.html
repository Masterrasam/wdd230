Our Promise

The Temple Inn & Suites is a hotel chain headquartered in Bethesda, MD. We build and manage specialized hotels near temple locations of The Church of Jesus Christ of Latter-day Saints around the world. Our hotels cater to temple workers, patrons, and their families by providing specialized services and accommodations that meet the needs of these groups. We also offer special services for youth groups, sealings, weddings, receptions, and childcare for temple patrons.

Amenities

Breakfast

Enjoy a piping hot breakfast delivered to your room every morning. Hotel Inn & Suites offers avariety of delectable healthy breakfasts, including parfaits, nuts and oats, toast, eggs, and more. (Items vary by location. Be sure to talk to your representative about any special dietary needs you may have.)

Changing Room Access

All patrons have special access to changing rooms and wash services at no additional cost.

Excerise Room

We care about our patrons' health, so we offer free excerise facilities for workouts and excerise to all our guests, free of charge.

Indoor Pool

Enjoy our relaxing indoor pool service, the perfect place to unwind after a long day.

Parking

Never fret with public parking garages again--our convenient parking location is reserved with your room, all within a comfortable distance of the Temple.

Rooms

Our guests enjoy clean, comfortably decorated rooms that are designed to promote an atmosphere of peace and godliness. We have several sized rooms available, capable of housing up to five people. We offer group discounts for wedding attendees. Rooms come with free WiFi high-speed internet access and smart TVs.

Temple Shuttle

When you stay with us and are attending the temple, we offer a free shuttle service too and from the temple. Just call the front desk to schedule a drop off or pick up.

Services

Playroom & Babysitting

Families can be together forever, but it doesn't always have to be hectic. Our facilities boast a cheerful playroom for your little ones, with short-term babysitting provided at a minimal fee.

Reception Area

Feel at peace in our Temple themed reception area, where you can relax, study, and more.

Family Research Center

All of our locations sport a state-of-the-art family research center with computer access and friendly experts to help you make the most of your stay. Even those who don't feel technologically savvy will be able to participate in the work of finding family names for Temple work. Our experts make it easy.
